## Feeder Data

### Format

**[Barangay Name]**

- [Feeder Name 1]
- [Feeder Name 2]

_Example:_
**Tabucan**

- Molo Feeder 4
- Megaworld Feeder 1

> Barangays can have "Portion of" and "Whole of". Just get the Barangay Name and then list the feeders they are under.
